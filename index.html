About

Bridging physical models and useful computation.

My work sits at the intersection of computational chemistry, machine learning, and scientific software engineering. I focus on methods that retain physical structure while making expensive molecular calculations more efficient and accessible.

The result is a research practice that moves between mathematical models, high-performance implementations, and tools scientists can use in real workflows.
